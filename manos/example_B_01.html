AI-Driven Engagement Strategies

Six Low-Cost, High-Impact Ideas for Donor-Patient Support

Journey Data Capture

Automated intake that remembers their story over time. Builds a profile so future interactions feel personal without human effort. One-time build, negligible ongoing cost.

Async Message Pool

Donor-patients record short voice/text messages that go into a moderated pool. Others can receive a random message of support. Batch moderation keeps costs minimal.

LLM Empathy Chat

A conversational AI that asks how they're doing, remembers context from previous chats, and offers warm, non-clinical support. Available 24/7 at pennies per conversation.

Automated Milestones

Triggered SMS/email at key dates (donation anniversary, diagnosis date) with personalized, AI-generated acknowledgment. Fully automated.

One-Question Check-In

Simple weekly prompt ("How are you feeling today?") via SMS. LLM responds with a brief, warm reply. Ultra-low friction, ultra-low cost.

AI Impact Update

Short, auto-generated message showing what their micro-donation contributed to, tailored to their history. Makes small contributions feel meaningful.
