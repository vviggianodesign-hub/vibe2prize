04

Suitability Framework

Dimension Ideal Fit (High Value) Poor Fit (High Risk)
UI/UX Focus High — Visual, interactive flows, front-end heavy apps. Low — Pure backend, data pipelines, or CLI tools.
Integration Low — Few external dependencies or standard APIs. High — Many legacy systems or complex middleware.
Regulatory Low — Internal tools, dashboards, non-critical apps. High — GxP, HIPAA, SOX, or financial systems.
Stakeholders High — Engaged, iterative client available for feedback. Low — Absent or slow-to-respond stakeholders.
Logic Complexity Low — Straightforward rules and standard patterns. High — Complex algorithms or proprietary math.
Core Philosophy

Qualification is not a gate to slow things down — it is a tool to focus effort where vibe coding creates the most value.

Qualification Checklist
[ ] Is the primary value in the user experience?
[ ] Are integration stubs acceptable for v1?
[ ] Is the client ready for 48h iteration cycles?
[ ] Does the team have hardening expertise?
