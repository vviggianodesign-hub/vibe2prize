PROHIBITED

Avoiding the Pitfalls of Typical AI Workshops

01

No Maturity Models

We won't waste time on abstract "AI maturity" levels. We focus on immediate, tangible delivery.

02

No Generic Case Studies

Forget what other industries are doing. We are here to solve YOUR specific R&D challenges.

03

No Architecture Diagrams

IT will present in plain language. We prioritize user value over technical complexity.

04

No Unchecked Wish-lists

Every R&D request is immediately vetted for feasibility. We only commit to what we can build.

05

No "Next Steps" Meetings

We don't end with a meeting request. We end with named owners and 30-day milestones.

Focus on specificity. Silence means agreement. Disagree openly.
