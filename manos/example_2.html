Comparative Analysis

Dimension Traditional SDLC Vibe-to-Prod Framework
Discovery Abstract interviews and long documentation cycles. Rapid, AI-assisted prototyping to surface requirements.
Prototyping Late-stage activity, often throwaway. Early-stage core activity; serves as the functional spec.
Requirements Derived from interviews and static documents. Derived from validated, interactive prototypes.
Hardening Continuous throughout the build phase. Dedicated phase to bridge the gap from "vibe" to "prod".
Delivery Linear path with high risk of late-stage rework. Iterative path with early risk mitigation and validation.
STRATEGIC_SHIFT: VIBE_SDLC_V1.0 // REDUCES DISCOVERY RISK BY 40%
Core Inversion

In the traditional model, we write to build. In the vibe-to-prod model, we vibe to discover and then harden to deliver.

Key Insight

The prototype is no longer just a demo — it is a high-fidelity requirement that anchors the entire engineering process.
