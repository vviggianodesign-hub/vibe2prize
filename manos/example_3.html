The Opportunity

Vibe coding offers a 40-60% reduction in initial development effort by automating boilerplate and accelerating the prototyping phase.

CRITICAL INSIGHT

Speed without structure creates Technical Debt at AI Scale. The goal is not to avoid vibe coding, but to govern it through a structured path to production.
